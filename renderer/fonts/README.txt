Bundled brand fonts (v0.2.7+):
  BebasNeue-Regular.woff2 - the tall display font used for FREQ.PHULL,
                            BPM/key readouts, metronome, etc.
  Inter-300.woff2         - Light text
  Inter-400.woff2         - Regular body text
  Inter-500.woff2         - Medium UI weight (buttons, labels)
  Inter-600.woff2         - Semibold (section headings)

Total payload: ~110 KB.

These are loaded via @font-face declarations at the top of index.html.
The app is fully offline-proof — no Google Fonts dependency.

Source:
  Bebas Neue : https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Bebas+Neue (SIL OFL 1.1)
  Inter      : https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter      (SIL OFL 1.1)
